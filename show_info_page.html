This Hip Hop re-boot of Romeo and Juliet is far from the usual. We decided to leave behind all the sadness and the traditonal aspects of the original Shakespeare story, and focus a bit more on the dance beats typical of Hip Hop sounds and rythm.

The entire show will be a musica and, along with acting performances, you will get to enjoy our songs made specifically for the show, recalling modern hip hop songs and some oldies. The story line will be slightly different, but we will not tell you too much yet. We want this to be an experience, not just a show to attend. It's going to be class.

Our team is based at the Velocity Dance Studio in Capitol Hill, Seattle, WA. A full crew of young dancers, singers is currently heading to Ireland as a first stop of a European Tour, following six months of a sold out one in the U.S. We believe in re-arranging what is known as the traditional drama and make it new with a modern approach. Get ready!
